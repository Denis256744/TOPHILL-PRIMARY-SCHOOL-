About Top Hill Primary School

Top Hill Primary School – Ssagala is dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where students excel academically, socially, and morally.

Our History

Founded in 1986, Top Hill Primary School has grown into a leading institution in Kyotera District. With a focus on discipline, innovation, and holistic development, we have nurtured hundreds of bright young minds who have gone on to excel in higher education and community leadership.
